3. Order Confirmation Process

After placing your order on our website, your order details are sent to us via WhatsApp. Our team will:

  • 1.Confirm your order via WhatsApp within 30 minutes during business hours (10 AM – 10 PM).
  • 2.Confirm the delivery address and timing.
  • 3.Notify you when your order is dispatched.
  • 4.Coordinate delivery at your convenience.

4. Payment on Delivery

Cash on Delivery (COD) is our primary payment method. Pay the full amount to our delivery person when your order arrives. UPI payment at delivery is also accepted. No advance payment is required for standard orders.

5. Large & Fragile Items

Some products — such as water fountains, sofas, large statues, and LED chandeliers — require special handling. For these items:

  • ✓Delivery is scheduled at a mutually convenient time.
  • ✓Our team assists with careful placement at your home.
  • ✓Please ensure access to your home is clear for large items.
  • ✓Inspect the item before accepting delivery and signing.

6. Damaged in Transit

If your item arrives damaged, please take photos immediately and WhatsApp them to us at +91 97891 82921 within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or resolution promptly.

7. Questions?

For any shipping or delivery queries, the fastest way to reach us is WhatsApp. We respond within 10 minutes during store hours.
