Our Story

Built for the Homes
of Virudhachalam

KGS Home Décors started with a simple frustration — people in Virudhachalam had to travel all the way to Chennai or Coimbatore to find quality home décor. We changed that.

Today, from our showroom on Junction Road, we stock over 500 carefully curated pieces — from handcrafted water fountains and statement sofas to elegant wall clocks, decorative statues, and premium gift items.

Every product is personally selected. Every piece meets our standard for quality, finish, and beauty. We believe your home deserves the best — and we bring it directly to you.
