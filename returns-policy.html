1. Return Window

You may request a return or exchange within 7 days of delivery for eligible items. To be eligible:

  • ✓Item must be unused and in original condition.
  • ✓Original packaging must be intact where possible.
  • ✓Return request must be initiated within 7 days via WhatsApp.

2. Eligible Reasons for Return

✓

Damaged on Delivery

Item arrived broken or physically damaged. Report within 24 hours with photos.

✓

Wrong Item Delivered

You received a different product than what you ordered.

✓

Manufacturing Defect

Item has a clear manufacturing or quality defect not caused by use.

✗

Change of Mind

Returns for change of mind are not accepted. However, we may offer an exchange at our discretion — please WhatsApp us to discuss.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • —Items that have been used, installed, or assembled.
  • —Customised or made-to-order items.
  • —Gift items once opened (unless defective).
  • —Items damaged after delivery due to misuse or improper handling.

4. Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available for eligible items within 7 days. If the replacement item costs more, the difference is payable. If it costs less, we refund the difference via UPI. Our team will coordinate the exchange pickup and delivery at no additional charge within Virudhachalam.

5. Refund Policy

Since most orders are Cash on Delivery, refunds (where applicable) are processed via UPI transfer within 3–5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. You will be informed via WhatsApp at each step.

6. Contact for Returns

The fastest way to initiate a return or exchange is via WhatsApp. Please have your order details and photos ready. We will guide you through the process personally.
